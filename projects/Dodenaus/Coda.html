Coda 2.0 (☐)

An interpretation of the astrophysical models of space by Rembert Dodoens. As described in his "De sphaera, sive de astronomiae et geographiae principiis cosmographica isagoge" he mentioned the existence of an alternative Geocentric model.

Made for the Dodenaus Digital Arts Festival in Mechelen
