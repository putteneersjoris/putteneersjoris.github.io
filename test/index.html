// Software Skills

I use SideFX Houdini for procedural and non-procedural work and the generation of synthetic datasets. Houdini has been my main driver for 6 years. When working with geometric models, I mainly output renders from the native houdini renderengines Karma or Mantra.
My Houdini expertise extends to SOP (surface operators), VOP (vector operators), LOP (lighting operators), PDG (procedural dependency graph), CHOP (channel operators) & COP (composite operators) contexts. I use vex (based on C++) or python as a way of optimizing and automating node structures and to express computational Logic inside the Houdini Framework.
I use Blender for visualisation, world building, art direction and prototyping. It is a fast, intuitive and versatile mesh based modeling software that allows me to rapidly sketch possible outcomes of projects.
